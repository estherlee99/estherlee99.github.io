ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Esther Lee. I am currently a senior at New Trier High School. As my first project, I created this website. I have interest in pursuing Computer Science and learning more about AI because I strongly believe in the potential of technoloy. Everyday, people are finding new, innovative ways to use technology and computer programming for the greater good. I hope that by studying Computer Science, I can be exposed to inspiration that may instigate revolutionary contributions.

In my free time, I love pondering, learning, and creating. My curiosity never fails me; I constantly find new things I want to understand or contemplate. When I am not thinking, I am creating. I often spend hours meticulously making and editing small films. And with every video, I gain new insights about many things. I also like composing lyrics or songs using different medias or instruments. Music has had a great impact on me, and continues to play a large role in my life. I like drawing, as well.These creations allow me to discover and exercise my originality. As a result, the products represent a slice of who I am. And I hope that this website can do the same.
